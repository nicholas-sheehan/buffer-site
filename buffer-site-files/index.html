OUR STORY

Buffer is a music rights agency and consultancy helping up-and-coming artists and songwriters get their fair share.

We’re here to help you find and collect the earnings from your work, so you can keep creating it.

We’re not a publisher, we’re a partner.

It’s a fresh approach to managing music rights that’s simple, straight-forward and fair.

What we do

Artists

From working with APRA AMCOS and PPCA to navigating your next big deal.

At Buffer we take a magnifying glass to your catalogue and make sure your releases are registered right.

Clumsy administration practices stop the payment process in its tracks - so we believe in the power of metadata to get your royalties flowing.

We look at your income streams and statements to make sure you don't miss out on any royalties. We know how to work the system to get you paid.

We think it's our job to help you understand the value in protecting your copyright.

We show you how to keep track of your assets. That's anything from the song you wrote today or the recording you release tomorrow, and remixes and features too.

We can translate your contracts so your obligations are clear. And if you have a new deal on the table, we can help you negotiate to get the best deal for you.

Managers

Maximise your artist roster and find new income streams.

Buffer can manage your contracts and licensing, and administer the copyright behind the songs.

We can consult and manage relationships with APRA AMCOS and PPCA, diving through each artists catalogue to boost existing income streams and find new ones. Getting more for you, and your artists.

We thrive on process and can help you build yours. We work on schedules and audit statements, providing a comprehensive overview of copyright assets and their value.

We know the ins-and-outs of contracts and can help you identify a good opportunity from a not-so-good one. We can help you with clearances too. From publishing to samples and synch to remixes, we've got you covered.

Don't be afraid to ask for our input - we'll be honest and transparent along the way.
